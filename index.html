Truck Support Venlo

Truck Support Venlo, based in the Netherlands, specializes in comprehensive maintenance and repair services for trucks and trailers. Under the Tire Support Venlo brand, the company offers advanced tire services, including procurement, installation, and repair. The company's mission is to ensure the safety and optimal performance of trucks and trailers through reliable maintenance solutions.

My graduation project aims to develop a full-stack application to enhance the tire procurement process for Truck Support Venlo. This tool will streamline tire purchasing, provide detailed comparisons of prices and quality, and improve overall operational efficiency. By using flutter for the frontend, Python for the backend, and advanced web scraping techniques, the application will offer a user-friendly and effective solution for warehouse managers.

Project Overview
Project Context and Justification

During my internship at Truck Support Venlo, I was tasked with addressing a significant challenge that impacts the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of their operationsâ€”tire procurement for trucks. Unlike the car tire sector, which benefits from a variety of advanced analytical tools, the truck tire sector suffers from a noticeable gap in similar technologies. This disparity has left warehouse managers at Truck Support without the necessary tools to make informed purchasing decisions, relying on fragmented and often outdated data to compare tire prices and quality.

Project Goal

The primary goal of this project was to develop a straightforward yet powerful software tool designed to streamline the tire buying process for warehouse managers. By providing a comprehensive comparison of tire suppliers along with actionable buying advice, the software aims to ensure that warehouse managers can procure high-quality tires at the best possible prices. This, in turn, enhances operational efficiency and drives cost savings across the organization.

Project Scope

The project scope was clearly defined to focus exclusively on the procurement of truck tires. Key features included:

  • Market Research: Conducting extensive market research to gather accurate and current data on tire prices and supplier reliability.
  • Scalable Application: Developing a scalable application that could adapt to growing data and user needs without degradation in performance.
  • Development of Scraping Bots: Creating sophisticated web scraping bots that automate the collection of tire data from various online sources, ensuring the data is current and comprehensive.

Conversely, the project was scoped with specific exclusions to maintain focus and manage resources effectively:

  • The application would not support the procurement of other types of goods or services.
  • It would not facilitate direct negotiations or transactions between warehouse managers and suppliers.
  • The focus would remain on suppliers within a specific region or country, excluding global market data unless relevant and feasible.
Constraints and Conditions

The development of the project was underpinned by several conditions:

  • A research paper would be prepared to document the findings and methodologies used.
  • An application architecture that includes both frontend and backend components would be developed, along with a working demo.
  • Technical assistance would be available as needed to address any complex challenges that might arise during development.

Project Methodology

Agile ScrumBan Methodology

Throughout the project, I employed an Agile ScrumBan methodology, a flexible hybrid approach that combines the structured elements of Scrum with the adaptability of Kanban. This approach allowed for dynamic planning and execution in response to evolving project requirements, ideal for the development of a software tool aimed at improving operational efficiencies at Truck Support Venlo. Key components included:

  • Continuous Refinement: Starting with intensive requirement gathering, where potential users were engaged and market research conducted, the product backlog was regularly updated to reflect emerging insights and needs.
  • Iterative Design and Development: System design was approached iteratively, allowing for adjustments based on user feedback and evolving project scopes. Development proceeded in cycles, integrating new features and improvements progressively.
Tools and Technologies

A modern and efficient set of tools and technologies were chosen to ensure robust development and scalability of the solution:

MySQL: Employed as the relational database to manage and store all project data efficiently, ensuring high performance and scalability.

Python: Utilized for backend services, with libraries such as BeautifulSoup, Scrapy, Selenium, and PyQuery for robust data scraping functionalities.
